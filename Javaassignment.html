Harry Potter and the Philospher's stone
Adaptation of the first of J.K. Rowling's popular children's novels about Harry Potter, a boy who learns on his eleventh birthday that he is the orphaned son of two powerful wizards and possesses unique magical powers of his own. He is summoned from his life as an unwanted child to become a student at Hogwarts, an English boarding school for wizards. There, he meets several friends who become his closest allies and help him discover the truth about his parents' mysterious deaths.

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
Harry Potter meets Dobby, a house-elf who warns him that it is dangerous to return to Hogwarts. Ron and Hermione rescue him in their father's flying car. In Diagon Alley, Harry sees Draco Malfoy's father slip a book into Ginny Weasley's cauldron. Harry, Ron, and Hermione plan to question Malfoy, using a polyjuice potion brew in a bathroom haunted by Moaning Myrtle. When Harry communicates with a snake, the school believes he is the heir, but Harry and Ron learn that a Muggle-born girl died when the Chamber was last opened. Harry and Ron find an enchanted diary owned by Tom Riddle, who blames Rubeus Hagrid for the Chamber's opening. When the diary is stolen and Hermione is petrified, Harry and Ron question Hagrid. Hagrid's giant pet spider, Aragog, reveals Hagrid's innocence. The school staff learns that Ginny has been taken into the Chamber and nominate Lockhart to save her. Harry enters the Chamber alone and finds Ginny unconscious, guarded by Riddle. Riddle uses the diary to manipulate Ginny into reopening the Chamber. Harry stabs the diary with the basilisk fang, destroying Riddle and reviving Ginny. Fawkes' tears heal Harry, who returns to Hogwarts with his friends and a baffled Lockhart. The basilisk's victims are healed, Hermione reunites with Harry and Ron, and Hagrid is released from Azkaban.

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry Potter returns to Hogwarts after spending a dissatisfying summer with the Dursleys. He learns that Sirius Black, a convicted supporter of Lord Voldemort, has escaped Azkaban Prison and intends to kill him. The Hogwarts Express is boarded by Dementors, who cause Harry to faint. Rubeus Hagrid is appointed Care of Magical Creatures teacher, but his first class goes awry. Harry sneaks into Hogsmeade and learns that Black is his godfather, but divulges the Potters' whereabouts to Voldemort.

To ward off the Dementors, Harry convinces Lupin to teach him the Patronus Charm. After Divination class, he witnesses Professor Trelawney enter a trance and prophesies that the Dark Lord will return. Snape arrives to apprehend Black, but is knocked out by Harry's Disarming spell. Lupin and Black reveal that Scabbers is the Animagus form of Pettigrew, who betrayed Harry's parents and framed Black for his own crimes. Dementors attack Black and Harry, but an unseen figure casts a powerful Patronus.

Harry and Hermione use Hermione's Time-Turner to go three hours back in time and set Buckbeak free from execution. Harry and Hermione free Black and return to the infirmary. Lupin resigns from teaching and returns the Marauder's Map to Harry.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

The Weasleys invite Harry Potter to attend the Quidditch World Cup final. The match ends in a victory for the Irish, but the campsite is attacked by Voldemort's followers. Hogwarts reopens and a new tournament is announced between three schools to compete for the Triwizard Cup. The Goblet of Fire is unveiled and selects Harry as a fourth champion, despite him being underage. Harry and Cedric find a partner, Parvati and Padma Patil, but Ron gets jealous of Hermione Granger with Viktor Krum.

The Quidditch field is prepared for the Third Task, which requires champions to overcome a maze filled with obstacles. Harry and Cedric succeed in reaching the centre, but are transported outside Hogwarts to a graveyard. Harry is alerted when his scar starts burning wildly, and Wormtail kills Cedric with his wand. Voldemort summons his Death Eaters and tries to kill Harry, but fails when his and Harry's wandstreams connect in Priori Incantatem. Dumbledore, McGonagall and Snape force Moody to reveal himself to be Barty Crouch Jr, a Death Eater formerly presumed dead.

Harry wins the Tournament and is transported to the graveyard for Voldemort's ritual. Harry recovers in the hospital wing and returns to live with the Dursleys for the summer.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix
Harry Potter and Dudley are attacked by Dementors and Harry repels them using a Patronus spell. The Ministry of Magic expels him from Hogwarts, but he is later exonerated. The Order of the Phoenix informs Harry that Lord Voldemort is seeking an object he previously lacked. Dolores Umbridge is appointed as Hogwarts' new Defence Against the Dark Arts professor and Harry is forced to write lines for "lying" about Voldemort. Ron and Hermione help Harry form "Dumbledore's Army" and Umbridge recruits Slytherins for an Inquisitorial Squad. Harry experiences a vision that Voldemort is torturing Sirius, and Harry, Ron, and Hermione rush to Umbridge's office to alert the Order via the Floo Network. Umbridge catches them and Hermione claims Dumbledore has hidden a "secret weapon" in the Forbidden Forest. Harry, Hermione, Ron, Luna, Neville and Ginny fly to the Ministry of Magic on Thestrals to save Sirius. They enter the Department of Mysteries and recover a bottled prophecy labelled with Harry's name. Death Eaters, including Lucius Malfoy and Bellatrix Lestrange, ambush them and force Harry to surrender the prophecy. Sirius and Remus Lupin arrive with Order members Nymphadora Tonks, Kingsley Shacklebolt and Mad-Eye Moody. Voldemort appears, but Dumbledore arrives and a violent duel erupts, destroying much of the Atrium. Voldemort is driven out by Harry's love for his friends and family. Umbridge is dismissed and Dumbledore returns as Hogwarts headmaster.

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Lord Voldemort tightens his grip on the wizarding and Muggle worlds and chooses Draco Malfoy to carry out a secret mission at Hogwarts. Harry Potter accompanies Albus Dumbledore to persuade former Potions professor Horace Slughorn to return to Hogwarts. Harry discovers that his used Potions textbook is filled with helpful notes and spells added by the "Half-Blood Prince". Ron makes the Gryffindor Quidditch team as Keeper and begins dating Lavender Brown, upsetting Hermione. Harry consoles Hermione while acknowledging his own feelings for Ginny Weasley.

At Hogwarts, Dumbledore asks Harry to retrieve Slughorn's memory of a young Voldemort. After Ron accidentally ingests a love potion intended for Harry, Harry takes him to Slughorn for a cure. Ron is poisoned upon sipping it and Harry confronts Draco about the poisoned mead Harry uses one of the Half-Blood Prince's curses without knowing what it is, injuring Malfoy. Ginny persuades Harry to hide the book in the Room of Requirement. Dumbledore and Harry learn Voldemort sought information about Horcruxes, magical objects containing pieces of a wizard's soul for immortality.

They travel to a cave where Harry aids Dumbledore in drinking a potion that hides another Horcrux, Slytherin's locket. As Hogwarts students and staff mourn Dumbledore's death, Harry tells Ron and Hermione that the locket is fake and contains a message from "R.A.B.", who stole the real Horcrux. Harry, Ron and Hermione agree to forgo their final Hogwarts year to hunt for the remaining Horcruxes.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 1
Severus Snape meets with Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters at Malfoy Manor. Harry survives Voldemort's attack, but Mad-Eye Moody and Hedwig are killed. During preparations for Bill Weasley and Fleur Delacour's wedding, the new Minister for Magic informs Harry, Ron, and Hermione of Albus Dumbledore's bequests, including a Deluminator, a copy of The Tales of Beedle the Bard, and the Golden Snitch. They escape to Number 12, Grimmauld Place and recover the locket, but are chased by Death Eaters in a chaotic exit. Hermione discovers a strange symbol drawn in Beedle the Bard that is identical to one Luna Lovegood's father Xenophilius wears. Harry and Hermione search for the sword in Godric's Hollow and encounter the same symbol in a cemetery. Bathilda Bagshot morphs into Voldemort's snake Nagini and attacks Harry. Harry, Ron, and Hermione visit Xenophilius Lovegood and learn the symbol represents the Deathly Hallows. During a vision, Harry sees an elderly Grindelwald telling Voldemort that the Elder Wand is buried with Dumbledore. At Malfoy Manor, Bellatrix Lestrange sees a Snatcher has Gryffindor's sword, and Harry begs for help using a mirror shard. Dobby helps save everyone while Harry grabs their captured wands from Draco Malfoy. Voldemort retrieves the Elder Wand from Dumbledore's tomb.
